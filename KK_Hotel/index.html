Overview

“The first KK Hotel with 65 rooms is located in the heart of Kajang.

The Hotel is easily accessible via the Grand Saga Highway from Cheras (PLUS) and North South highway (SILK) and the Middle Ring Road II (MRR2). Only 20 km from the city center, the hotel's strategic location ensures that guests can quickly and easily reach many local points of interest. With its convenient location, the hotel offers easy access to the city's must-see destinations, 30-minutes’ drive from Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA) and Kuala Lumpur city center. The facilities and services provided by Hotel KK Kajang ensure a pleasant stay for guests. Come and explore more about KK Hotel.
